Gujarati Club

The Gujarati Club of VIT Bhopal is a vibrant and dynamic student club dedicated to celebrating and promoting Gujarati culture. Through a variety of cultural events, we showcase the richness and diversity of Gujarat’s traditions, art, music, and dance. Additionally, our club actively engages in community service initiatives to give back to society and create a positive impact. Join us to be part of this cultural extravaganza, where we come together to embrace and preserve the essence of Gujarat at the Gujarati Club!

Bengali Club

The Bengali Club at VIT Bhopal University is vibrant community celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Bengal. Join us to immerse yourself in the language, art, music, and traditions of Bengal. Our club organizes cultural events, workshops, and celebrations of Bengali festivals, fostering a sense of belonging and promoting cultural exchange. Whether you’re a native Bengali speaker or curious about Bengal’s beauty, all are welcome to experience the warmth and diversity of Bengali culture. Let’s unite in our love for Bengal and create cherished memories at the Bengali Club!
